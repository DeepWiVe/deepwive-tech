End-to-end optimized wireless video solution.

Traditional video communication pipelines are prone to disconnect in unstable wireless environments due to the modular approach, leading to multiple failure ponts.

DeepWiVe vertically integrates the entire transmission pipeline, optimizing each component end-to-end, using state-of-the-art Deep Learning methods.

Use Cases

Drones

DeepWiVe can enable drones to fly further without losing video feed and thanks to the parallelizability of Deep Neural Networks, the energy efficiency is much greater than conventional approaches, allowing the drone to fly further and longer.

Wireless VR/AR

No one likes to be tethered to a bulky gaming PC when they are enjoying the metaverse. DeepWiVe is capable of delivering <1ms video latency, making it uniquely suitable for uncompromized wireless VR/AR experiences.
